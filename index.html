Speakers

Sunset Sounds' speakers are designed and engineered with immaculate care and detail. Our mission statement is to bring high fidelity sound to the portable speaker market... but with a twist. Every speaker is locally handmade with unparalleled attention to detail and product finish. Oh, and did we mention? They're 3D printed to allow for a distinctive modern aesthetic , unique grain patterns in 3D printed wood and product finishes, and special filament choice for recyclability/biodegradability.
